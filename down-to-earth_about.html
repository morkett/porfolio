Life At Down To Earth Starts With A Gorgeous Breakfast Selection Of Super Energizing Bowls And Treats.

At 12:00, The Lunch Menu Is Set To Impress Even The Most Carnivorous Of Tastes.

Throughout The Day We Offer An Impressive Selection Of Raw Cakes And Desserts Served With A Selection Of Organic, Biodynamic Teas Or Coffees.

All Raw Cakes Do Come With A Polite Warning – You Would Rarely Feel The Need To Eat A Conventional Cake Again!

For Afternoon Drinks, You May Wish To Indulge In A Selection Of Organic Wines And Beers Perfectly Accompanied With A Selection Of Small Plates To Share.
